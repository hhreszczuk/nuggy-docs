The types of Personal Information we collect will depend on how you use Nuggy, but may include:

- **Account and profile details**\
  Name, email address, role (e.g. shift manager, crew member), business name, location details for a venue, and any profile preferences you set.
- **Roster and shift information**\
  Shift times, positions / stations, break timing and status, team assignments, and related notes created through the app.
- **Contact and support information**\
  Your contact details, communications with us (support emails, feedback, in-app messages) and information you provide when you respond to surveys or beta programs.
- **Usage and device information**\
  IP address, device identifiers, browser type, app version, operating system, timestamps, and basic analytics about how you use the Services (for example, which screens are accessed and how frequently).
